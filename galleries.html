Art Galleries

Jehangir Art Gallery

It was founded by Sir Cowasji Jehangir at the urging of K.K. Hebbar and Homi Bhabha. The Jehangir Art Galery over the years is recognized nationally and internationally as a centre of Contemporary Indian Art.

Project 88

The cavernous space was once a office that housed employees of an elevator company. Luckily the six-year old gallery, owned by Sree Goswami, is now a minimalist space which bears little evidence of its industrial past.

Gallery Maskara

It has a clear and Compelling mission to taking a global and multidisciplinary approach to art that corresponds to the cultural fabric of our time thus fueling critical dialogue, collaboration and public engagement.

Gallery Of Modern Art

It was opened to the public in 1996. It hosts various exhibitions and art collections of famous artists, sculptors and different civilisations. It is located near Regal Cinema in Colaba.

Chemould Prescott Road

It was founded by Kekko Gandhy and Khorshed Gandhy in 1963, and is one of the oldest commercial art galleries in India. Chemould has been instrumental in establishing the reputations of many Indian modern artists.

Gallery Tasveer

It is an organisation commited to the art of photography and photography as art. Dedicated to promoting and showcasing contemporary photography, Tasveer has created a network of art galleries in India.
